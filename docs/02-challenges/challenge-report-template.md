# Sutherland Challenge Report Template

Use this template when creating a challenge report. Save as `docs/02-challenges/{project-name}-sutherland-challenge.md`.

## Project Challenge Report

### Rational Review
- Summarize the Business Analyst / Research logic (2–3 paragraphs). Call out key assumptions and decision levers.

### Behavioral Blind Spots
- Bullet the psycho-logical gaps with examples and brief explanation (use Rory-style quotes or short analogies).

### Counterintuitive Proposals
| Original Idea | Sutherland Twist | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| [BA Feature] | [Reframed Version] | [Psycho-Logic Gain] |

### Experiment Plans (one per proposal)
- Control: [current flow]
- Treatment: [reframe]
- Sample: [N or % traffic]
- Duration: [days/weeks]
- Primary metric: [single KPI]
- Success threshold: [target uplift]

### Risks of Rationality
- Short bullets on what data-driven thinking might miss and possible pitfalls.

### Approval Gates
- Does it change perception? ✅/❌
- Counterintuitive test passed? ✅/❌
- Ready for UX/UI? ✅/❌ (only if above checks pass)

### One-line Takeaway
- A memorable closing line, e.g., "Logic got us here; alchemy gets us ahead."
